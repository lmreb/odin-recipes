Garlic Confit Toast

Garlic confit spread on sliced baguette, with herbs and a bit of lemon zest.

Ingredients:

  • 1 head garlic
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 teaspoons chopped oregano
  • 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 baguette sliced in half lengthwise, then crosswise

Steps:

  1. Cook cloves from 1 head garlic in 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter in a small
    covered saucepan over medium-low heat until golden brown and very soft, 15â€“20 minutes.
    Transfer to a medium bowl; let cool.
  2. Add 1 cup grated Parmesan, 2 teaspoons chopped oregano, 1 teaspoon grated lemon zest,
    and 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes to garlic and mash to a paste; season with salt.
  3. Heat broiler. Slice a baguette in half lengthwise, then crosswise. Broil, cut side
    down, on a foil-lined baking sheet until golden brown, about 2 minutes (watch carefully).
    Let cool slightly, then spread cut side with garlic paste.
    Broil until cheese is golden and bubbling, about 2 minutes. Slice.
